This is form for tour reservation.

To reserve one tour you need to do following things:

Choose one voucher if you want by selecting it from the list.
You can deselect it by clicking button right to the vouchers list.

Enter number of guests for which you want to reserve, keep in mind that you
can't reserve for more guests than there are free spots on tour.
Next to field where the number of guests is entered you have many spots are left
for the current input.

Next you have to select who are concrete guests you are reservating for, if
you are reservating for someone except yourself.
Number of entered guests must be same to the guests number that you entered before,
otherwise system won't allow you to submit reservation.

Once you sure you have completed reservation, click on button make a reservation
and reservation will be saved.
