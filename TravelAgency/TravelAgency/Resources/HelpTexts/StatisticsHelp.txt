This is statistics of all tour requests that you have made.

In the upper part there is table which shows percentage of accepted
tour requests and average number of people in this accepted requests.

This is shown generally for all time.
And it is shown for specific year, to choose year select it from
combo box on the left side of the table.

Under this is graph which shows relation between made requests and
languages in this requests, or location.

You can choose statistic you want from the radio buttons (location or language)
on the left side of the graph.
