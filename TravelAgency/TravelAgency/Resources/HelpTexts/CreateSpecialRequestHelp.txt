Here you can create special tour request.

To create special tour request you just need to make at least
two ordinary request, same way as regular request is created.

The only difference is that when you finish one request, you just
click on save and add next request, and new form for new request will open.

Also you can go back and forward through your requests by clicking pointing
buttons on the left and right side.

You can discard the request which are you currently filling, by clicking delete this request.

Once you finished click on submit request and request will be saved and showed in
special requests page.
