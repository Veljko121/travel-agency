Here you can choose what tour to reserve.

In the table in the middle are all tours that are currently available.

To reserve tour click select one tour by clicking anywhere in the row of appropriate tour.

Then click on "make a reservation" button in bottom left corner.

You can filter table by multiple parameters (country, city...) by entering text in fields over the table.

If you want to see vouchers you have click on "My vouchers" button at the bottom right corner.
